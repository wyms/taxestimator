What You Should Know

In Scope (v1)

  • Filing Status: Single or Married Filing Jointly
  • Standard Deduction only
  • Federal income tax only
  • Multiple W-2 and paystub inputs
  • Tax due/refund estimates
  • PDF export and shareable links

Out of Scope (v1)

  • Other filing statuses (HoH, MFS, QW)
  • Tax credits (child tax, EITC, etc.)
  • State or local taxes
  • Itemized deductions
  • Self-employment income
  • Capital gains or investment income
